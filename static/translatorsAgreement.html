This agreement, between Universal Translation Services (hereinafter "UTS"), and THE TRANSLATOR (hereinafter "Independent Contractor" - "IC"), defines the respective rights, duties and obligations of the aforementioned parties in connection with any and all language services performed by IC on behalf of one or more of the clients of UTS, whether or not such services are performed at the behest of UTS.

WHEREAS, UTS is in the business of providing language services to individuals, business entities, and governmental agencies; and

WHEREAS, THE TRANSLATOR is a qualified language instructor / interpreter / translator (circle appropriate title) who wishes to undertake assignments in his/her specialty area(s) as an IC for UTS;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

1. GENERAL

IC understands and agrees that UTS will be utilizing his/her services only on an as-needed basis, based solely upon the demand of UTS's clients for UTS's services. Thus, UTS cannot guarantee any minimum amount of work. However connected to Tranwise you will get all jobs offered in your language combiantion which you have specified in Tranwise. Moreover, it is possible that there will be no demand whatsoever by UTS's clients for IC's language specialty during any specified period of time, and it is also possible that such demand as there is may not be sufficient to warrant UTS's contacting IC. In view of the foregoing, IC may, without penalty, decline to accept any offered assignment from UTS.

2. PROCEDURE

When UTS receives a request for language services from one of its clients requiring IC's language specialty, and wishes to engage IC's services, UTS will then offer the assignment to IC through Tranwise or by email, who may, as provided in Paragraph 1, above, decline to accept said assignment. If IC agrees to accept the assignment, he/she agrees to accept compensation in the manner and amount agreed in advance, and hereby further agrees to adhere fully to all internal UTS procedures, including quality control procedures, set forth in the Business Unit's SOP, as well as to all other terms and conditions outlined therein.

3. IC QUALIFICATIONS

UTS carefully selects and screens the persons to whom it gives consideration for service as UTS IC.

4. INTERNAL UTS PROCEDURES

IC agrees to adhere to all procedures outlined in the specific software Tranwise. In particular, IC agrees to submit job overview and payment per job by the end of the month according to the system Tranwise where all overview of jobs are shown. With regard to the maintenance of quality control by UTS, IC further agrees that he/she will submit to procedures outlined in Tranwise. Adherence by IC to the foregoing UTS internal procedures, including quality control procedures, is a material provision of this agreement. Because adherence to UTS's internal procedures is an integral part of IC's responsibilities, such adherence is not separately compensated or paid for by UTS, but is rather hereby expressly included in the UTS IC compensation set forth in Paragraph 5, below.

5. PRICE

All freefixed translators receive an word price according to the Tranwise system and specified in the Tranwise system In turn, UTS pays its IC mentioned in the Tranwise which IC receives, all jobs done in the month cummulated together in the first week of the next month.

6. PAYMENT

UTS agrees to pay IC his/her invoice in Euro's always in the first week of every month.

hereby expressly agrees that he/she will receive no monies whatsoever, whether in his or her own name or in the name of or on behalf of UTS, from UTS's client(s). Strict adherence to this provision is a material condition of this agreement, and any failure to observe this condition shall constitute a material breach of this agreement by IC. In the event of any such breach UTS shall have the right to immediately terminate this agreement, and to pursue such other legal remedies against IC as may be available.

8. PROHIBITION AGAINST DIRECT OR INDIRECT SOLICITATION OF UTS'S CLIENTS

It is a material provision of this agreement that IC hereby agrees not to directly solicit any of UTS's clients (expressly including those UTS clients or their employees for whom IC is providing or has provided language services for UTS), for a period of one (1) year from the last day of the IC's work for said UTS client on UTS's behalf. Any direct or indirect solicitation of UTS's clients by IC shall constitute a material breach of this agreement, grounds for immediate termination of said IC by UTS, and grounds for legal action by UTS against IC.

9. PROHIBITION AGAINST ACCEPTING A POSITION FROM UTS'S CLIENTS

It is a material provision of this agreement that IC hereby agrees not to accept a position in any capacity -- i.e. --from any of UTS's clients for a period of one (1) year from the last day of the IC's work for said UTS client on UTS's behalf. 10. TERMINATION

This agreement is terminable at will, by either party, with or without cause, and with or without notice of any kind. In particular, IC hereby acknowledges his/her understanding that his/her continued engagement by UTS is dependent upon both UTS's, and UTS's client's continuing satisfaction with his/her performance. Thus, IC is subject to termination as noted above in the event of dissatisfaction with his/her performance by UTS, or UTS's client, entirely without regard to whether or not such dissatisfaction is objectively warranted. N.B. In the event of termination by either party for any reason, Paragraphs 7, 8 and 9 of this Agreement shall remain in full force and effect, and binding upon IC, for a period of one year following the effective date of termination.

11. PAYMENT FOLLOWING TERMINATION IC

shall receive the agreed upon amount for all services rendered satisfactorily prior to the time of termination. Conversely, regardless of whether or not there was cause or notice for termination, IC shall not be entitled to any compensation, or other form of consideration of any kind, beyond payment for work actually performed satisfactorily on behalf of UTS pursuant to this agreement.

12. CONFIDENTIALITY

It is a material condition of this agreement that IC shall maintain strict confidentiality concerning the names of UTS's clients, the names of UTS's clients' clients, UTS's policies and procedures, and any information obtained during the performance of an assignment for UTS. Any failure by IC to comply with this provision shall be grounds for immediate termination.

13. HIPAA COMPLIANCE of IC

UTS may accept assignments containing protected health information (PHI) or electronic protected health information (ePHI) that is covered under the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act of 1996 (HIPAA). Any individually identifiable health information associated with an assignment shall be protected by IC during the performance of an assignment for UTS.

Data that is "individually identifiable" includes any piece of information that can be used, either alone or in combination, to identify an individual. Under HIPAA, Protected Health Information (PHI) is information that identifies an individual (in this case UTS' clients) and relates to the following:

  • The Client's past, present, or future physical or mental health
  • The provision of healthcare to the Client
  • The past, present, or future payment for health care
  • The Client's personally identifiable information (PII) including date of birth, policy number, address, telephone numbers, fax number, email address, social security number, medical record number, account number, certificate/license number, health plan beneficiary number
  • Any other unique identifying number, characteristic, or code.
All of the above identifiers about UTS' clients shall not be disclosed by IC, except as required by Law. Upon termination of the Agreement for any reason, IC shall destroy all Protected Health Information received from UTS, or created, maintained, or received by IC on behalf of Client that the IC still maintains in any form. IC shall retain no copies of the Protected Health Information.

14. NON-EXCLUSIVITY

Recognizing that UTS may not ever, or may only on an occasional basis, have an assignment to offer to IC, IC shall have the right to accept assignments from other companies; provided however, that IC nevertheless shall remain obligated to adhere to the provisions of this agreement concerning confidentiality and direct solicitation of UTS's clients.
